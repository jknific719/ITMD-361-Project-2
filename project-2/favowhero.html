My favorite hero in the game Overwatch is the hero Zarya.

Zarya's role, abilities, and stats

  • Role
    • Tank
  • Abilities
    • Energy: Boosts damage output of her Particle Cannon attacks.
    • Particle Cannon Beam: Fires a short-range beam of energy.
    • Particle Cannon Bomb: Fires a ball of energy that explodes on impact.
    • Particle Barrier: Generates a shield around Zarya that absorbs damage to create Energy.
    • Projected Barrier: Generates a shield around an ally that absorbs damage to create Energy.
    • Ultimate: Graviton Surge: Zarya launches a ball of energy that sucks enemies to its center, and deals damage over time.
  • Stats
    • Health: 200 pts
    • Shield: 200 pts
